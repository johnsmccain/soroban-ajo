Ajo – Dashboard & Transaction Experience

This project showcases the dashboard and transaction history flow I worked on for Ajo, a community-based savings platform.

My goal was to design a clear, data-focused experience that helps users track savings, monitor performance, and review transactions effortlessly.

What I Worked On

Analytics Dashboard

Transaction History

Financial data visualization

Activity tracking experience

Dashboard

I designed the dashboard to give users an immediate overview of their group’s financial health.

Focus Areas

Clear total savings display

Monthly growth indicator

Pot progress tracking

Next payout visibility

Contribution trend visualization

Member activity feed

Design priority: clarity, hierarchy, and quick insight.

Transaction History

I worked on simplifying transaction tracking while maintaining transparency.

Focus Areas

Search and date filtering

Activity categorization (All, Contributions, Payouts)

Clear status indicators (Completed, Pending, Failed)

Visual distinction between credit and debit

Export functionality

Design priority: easy scanning and financial confidence.

Design Approach

Minimal UI

Structured card layout

Consistent spacing system

Strong visual hierarchy

Dark mode optimization

Outcome

The experience helps users:

Understand their savings performance quickly

Track contributions and payouts with ease

Feel confident and informed about their financial activity
